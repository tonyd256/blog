A wine ecommerce company creating a new way to purchase and learn about wine through values and sommolier curated boxes of wine from small to medium sized wineries. I worked closely with a design team at Akufen to build their ecommerce platform on Wordpress and WooCommerce.

A website to host a podcast about running far and "the why" behind it. Worked closely with a freelance designer to build this JAMstack website. JAMstack allows us to build a great looking website with stellar SEO performance.

A simple website to announce a running challenge in the Bay Area and to collect people's time submissions. I used a pre-existing style and Google Sheets as a backend to spin up a quick, functioning, low cost, website.
